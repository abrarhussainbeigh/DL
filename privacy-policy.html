Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

Welcome to DeviceLinker, a device management tool designed to provide ethical and legal monitoring solutions. Your privacy and data security are our priority. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your data when you use our services.

By using our app, you agree to the terms outlined in this policy.

2. User Consent & Installation Responsibility

Our Android app requires explicit user consent post-installation before any functionality is activated.

No data collection occurs until consent is granted after installation.

3. Data We Collect

Once user consent is obtained, we may collect the following data from connected devices:

A. Device & User Information

  • Device model, OS version
  • SIM card details (including number & carrier information)
  • Contact list & call logs (if access is granted)

B. Files & Media

  • Uploaded files (up to a limited size)
  • Photos & videos stored on the device (if enabled)

C. Usage & Activity Data

  • Real-time device management logs
  • IP address and system logs for security monitoring
  • Location data (if tracking is enabled by the user)

4. How We Use Your Data

Collected data is used strictly for:

  • Device management & security features
  • Providing real-time monitoring and alerts
  • Enhancing service performance & troubleshooting
  • Ensuring compliance with legal regulations

We do not sell, rent, or misuse personal data for advertising.

5. Data Storage & Protection

Encryption: Some data is stored in encrypted format, while others remain unencrypted as per functionality requirements.

Secure Storage: Data is stored in secure cloud infrastructure with restricted access.

Access Control: Only authorized systems can retrieve stored data.

Security Enhancements: We continuously upgrade security features with every update.

6. Data Retention Policy

We retain data only as long as necessary to provide our services.

Users can request deletion of their stored data at any time.

Some system logs may be retained for security and compliance.

7. Third-Party Services

We use secure cloud-based services for backend operations.

Some services process data as part of their functionality, ensuring strict security standards.

No personal data is shared for marketing or unauthorized purposes.

8. Data Breach & Liability Disclaimer

While we implement strong security measures, we cannot guarantee absolute data security.

In case of a data breach, affected users will be notified as per legal regulations.

DeviceLinker is not responsible for any unforeseen data leaks or unauthorized access beyond our control.

9. User Rights Under GDPR & Other Privacy Laws

Users have the right to:

  • Access their data stored in our system.
  • Request corrections to incorrect or outdated data.
  • Request deletion of their data (Right to be Forgotten).
  • Restrict data processing for specific use cases.
  • Transfer their data to another service provider.
  • Object to certain types of data processing.

10. Changes to This Policy

We update our Privacy Policy regularly to reflect changes in security practices and compliance requirements. Users will be notified of significant changes.
